## RESTRICTED STOCK PURCHASE AGREEMENT

This Restricted Stock Purchase Agreement (this "Agreement") is made as of {{FILING_DATE}} (the "Date of Issuance"), by and between **{{COMPANY_NAME}}**, a {{STATE}} corporation (the "Company"), and **{{STOCKHOLDER_NAME}}** (the "Purchaser").

### RECITALS

A. The Company's Board of Directors has authorized the issuance of shares of the Company's stock to the Purchaser, subject to the terms and conditions of this Agreement.

B. The Purchaser desires to acquire shares of the Company's stock, subject to the terms set forth herein.

### AGREEMENT

**1. PURCHASE AND SALE OF SHARES**

1.1 **Issuance.** Subject to the terms of this Agreement, the Company hereby issues to the Purchaser, and the Purchaser hereby accepts, **{{SHARES}}** shares of {{STOCK_CLASS}} of the Company (the "Shares") at a purchase price of **${{PAR_VALUE}} per share** (the "Purchase Price"), for a total purchase price of **${{TOTAL_PRICE}}**.

1.2 **Consideration.** The Purchase Price shall be paid by the Purchaser in the form of: {{CONSIDERATION}}.

**2. VESTING**

2.1 **Vesting Schedule.** The Shares shall vest according to the following schedule:

- **Vesting Period:** {{VESTING_PERIOD}} (e.g., 4 years)
- **Cliff:** {{CLIFF_PERIOD}} (e.g., 1 year)
- **Vesting Start Date:** {{FILING_DATE}}

{{VESTING_PERCENTAGE}}% of the Shares shall vest on the {{CLIFF_PERIOD}} anniversary of the Vesting Start Date (the "Cliff Date"). Thereafter, the remaining Shares shall vest in equal monthly installments over the remaining {{REMAINING_VESTING}} months, such that 100% of the Shares will be vested on the {{VESTING_PERIOD}} anniversary of the Vesting Start Date.

2.2 **Unvested Shares.** Unvested Shares remain subject to the Company's Repurchase Option (Section 3).

**3. COMPANY REPURCHASE OPTION**

3.1 **Repurchase Right.** In the event the Purchaser's service to the Company terminates for any reason (whether voluntary or involuntary, with or without cause), the Company shall have the right (but not the obligation) to repurchase any or all of the Purchaser's then-unvested Shares at a price equal to the original Purchase Price per share (${{PAR_VALUE}}).

3.2 **Exercise Period.** The Company may exercise this Repurchase Option within 90 days after the Purchaser's termination of service by delivering written notice to the Purchaser.

3.3 **Lapse.** The Repurchase Option shall lapse with respect to Shares as they vest pursuant to Section 2.

**4. TRANSFER RESTRICTIONS**

4.1 **No Transfer.** The Purchaser shall not sell, assign, transfer, pledge, hypothecate, or otherwise dispose of any Shares (or any interest therein) except:
   (a) to the Company pursuant to the Repurchase Option;
   (b) with the prior written consent of the Board of Directors; or
   (c) by will or the laws of descent and distribution.

4.2 **Right of First Refusal.** Before any transfer of vested Shares, the Company shall have a right of first refusal to purchase the Shares on the same terms offered by any bona fide third-party purchaser.

**5. SECTION 83(b) ELECTION**

5.1 **Election.** The Purchaser acknowledges that the Purchaser has been advised to consult with a tax advisor regarding the advisability of filing an election under Section 83(b) of the Internal Revenue Code with respect to the Shares. **The Purchaser understands that any such election must be filed with the IRS within thirty (30) days of the Date of Issuance.**

5.2 **Company Not Responsible.** The Company shall not be responsible for filing the Purchaser's 83(b) election or for any failure by the Purchaser to make a timely filing.

**6. REPRESENTATIONS**

6.1 The Purchaser represents that the Shares are being acquired for investment purposes only and not with a view to distribution or resale.

6.2 The Purchaser is an "accredited investor" as defined in Rule 501 of Regulation D under the Securities Act of 1933, or has such knowledge and experience in financial and business matters that the Purchaser is capable of evaluating the merits and risks of this investment.

6.3 The Purchaser understands that the Shares have not been registered under the Securities Act and are "restricted securities" within the meaning of Rule 144.

**7. LEGENDS**

All certificates or book entries representing the Shares shall bear the following legend:

> "THE SHARES REPRESENTED HEREBY HAVE NOT BEEN REGISTERED UNDER THE SECURITIES ACT OF 1933, AS AMENDED, AND MAY NOT BE SOLD, TRANSFERRED, OR OTHERWISE DISPOSED OF WITHOUT REGISTRATION OR AN APPLICABLE EXEMPTION FROM REGISTRATION. THE SHARES ARE SUBJECT TO A RESTRICTED STOCK PURCHASE AGREEMENT BETWEEN THE COMPANY AND THE STOCKHOLDER, A COPY OF WHICH IS ON FILE WITH THE SECRETARY OF THE COMPANY."

**8. GENERAL PROVISIONS**

8.1 **Governing Law.** This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of {{STATE}}.

8.2 **Entire Agreement.** This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties with respect to the subject matter hereof.

8.3 **Amendment.** This Agreement may be amended only by a written instrument signed by both parties.

8.4 **Severability.** If any provision of this Agreement is held to be invalid, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.
